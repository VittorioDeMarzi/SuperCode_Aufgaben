Decorating with plants in simple

To create the most intresting display, use a mixture of plants sizes and leafs shapes. A large plant like Pippa the peace lily, mixed with something sculptural like Susie the snake plant and a soft fern like Bertie gives an attractive range of textures. Hanging plants, like Rapunzel the golden photos, add interest at different heights. Don't forget your pots. Matching pots will look clean and elegant, while a mix of styles and patterns looks eclectic and fun.
